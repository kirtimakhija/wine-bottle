BUY EIKTYRNE WHISKY

Where to buy in Norway

For the moment we only distribute our Eiktyrne Whisky in Norway. Vinmonopolet is a government-owned alcoholic beverage retailer and the only company allowed to sell beverages containing an alcohol content higher than 4.75% in Norway.

See our current Eiktyrne assortment here

For the ontrade industry our Eiktyrne Whisky is available through all major wholesalers such as Vectura, Vinhuset, ASKO or Servicegrossistene.

Starting in 2021, Eiktyrne whisky will be available in Sweden, Finland, Denmark, Germany and hopefully in other markets. More info to come.
